What is Codemania?

Codemania v5.0 is the fifth year of the 12-hour night hackathon proudly organized by the IEEE Computer Society Student Branch at SLTC. This exciting event invites SLTC undergraduates to unleash their coding prowess, tackle real-world challenges, and collaborate. Designed to be more than a competition, Codemania serves as a vibrant platform to showcase talent, foster problem-solving excellence, and foster a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts. The passion for coding meets the value of the field, empowering the next generation of technology leaders to shine and excel in the ever-evolving digital world.

Why should you choose Codemania v5.0?

  • Compete in diverse challenges ranging from logic puzzles to algorithmic programming.
  • Work with like-minded individuals and create innovative solutions.
  • Gain recognition, win awards, and grow your professional skills.

Rules and Regulations

Teaming

  • 1-3 members per team.
  • One Team Leader for coordination.
  • Each participant can belong to only one team.

Competition Rules

  • Challenges range from beginner to advanced.
  • All solutions must be based on your knowledge. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  • Judges' decisions are final.
