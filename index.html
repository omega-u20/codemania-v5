What is CodeMania?

Welcome to CodeMania V5.0, Where innovation meets competition. Hosted by the IEEE Computer Society Student Branch Chapter of SLTC, CodeMania V5.0 is a 12-hour overnight hackathon designed for SLTC undergraduates to showcase their coding talents, solve real-world challenges, and connect with peers.

Key Features:

  • Exclusive for SLTC Undergraduates
  • 12 Hours of Coding
  • Prestigious Prizes
  • Exciting Challenges

Why should you choose CodeMania V5.0?

  • Challenge: Compete in diverse challenges ranging from logic puzzles to algorithmic programming.
  • Collaborate: Work with like-minded individuals and create innovative solutions.
  • Achieve: Gain recognition, win awards, and grow your professional skills

Rules

Team Formation

  • 1-3 members per team.
  • One Team Leader for coordination.
  • Each participant can belong to only one team.

Competition Rules

  • Challenges range from beginner to advanced.
  • All solutions must be based on your knowledge. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  • Judges' decisions are final.
