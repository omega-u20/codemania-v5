What is Codemania?

  Codemania v5.0 is the fifth installment of our 12-hour- long algorithmic coding hackathon proudly organized by the IEEE Computer Society Student Branch Chapter of SLTC, designed to highlight your exceptional coding skills.

Why you should compete in Codemania v5.0?

  • Provide a platform for an in-person coding competition for programmers to tackle diverse and challenging coding problems.
  • Offer professional growth opportunities in the IT industry.
  • Celebrate and reward outstanding talent through prestigious awards, inspiring participants to strive for technical and creative excellence.

Rules and Regulations

Teaming

  • Maximum 3 participants per team.
  • One Team Leader for coordination.
  • Each participant can belong to only one team.

Registering

  • Competitors must complete registration before 11:59 pm on January 24th, using the provided form.
  • Participation is open to undergraduate students of SLTC , and IEEE membership is not required.
  • Only registered teams are allowed to participate.
  • Team members must present email confirmations received during the registration process.
  • Registrations begin at 4:00 p.m., and participants must arrive at least 10 minutes before the start.

Competition

  • Competitors must attend physically.
  • Challenges will be categorized into easy, intermediate, and hard levels.
  • Participants must use provided HackerRank accounts and rename them as instructed.
  • Challenges must be completed during the given time.
  • Competitors can make multiple attempts with no penalties for incorrect answers.
  • A valid submission must score higher than previous attempts for specific challenges.
  • Competitors must solve challenges without seeking help from individuals outside their team.
  • Use of internet searches is acceptable, but directly copying code is prohibited.
  • All code submitted will undergo plagiarism checks.
  • Failure to adhere to rules results in immediate disqualification.

Scoring & Winners

  • Winners selected based on HackerRank platform's scoring mechanism.
  • Cash prizes awarded to the top 3 teams.
  • Decisions of judges are final and absolute.
